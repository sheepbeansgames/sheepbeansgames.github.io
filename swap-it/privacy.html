1. 概覽1. Overview

「該換了吧」是由 SheepBeansGames 開發的行動應用程式，協助你追蹤家中耗材的更換週期、備品庫存、採購清單與相關提醒。

Swap It. is a mobile app developed by SheepBeansGames to help you track replacement schedules, household inventory, shopping lists, and related reminders.

核心物品資料以本機儲存為主。部分功能，例如雲端備份、意見回饋、登入、匿名分析與訂閱狀態，會使用 Firebase、Apple App Store 或 Google Play 相關服務。

Core item data is primarily stored on your device. Some features, such as cloud backup, feedback, sign-in, anonymous analytics, and subscription status, use Firebase, Apple App Store, or Google Play services.

2. 本機儲存的資料2. Data Stored Locally

以下資料主要儲存在你的裝置本機，不會在未經你操作的情況下自動上傳：

The following data is primarily stored locally on your device and is not automatically uploaded without your action:

  • 你建立的空間名稱、分類結構與排列順序
  • 物品名稱、更換週期、到期日、安全庫存量
  • 你拍攝或選取的物品照片，以及去背後產生的貼紙圖片
  • 更換、補貨、延後等操作紀錄與使用統計
  • 採購清單內容與應用程式設定
  • Space names, category structure, and ordering you create
  • Item names, replacement cycles, due dates, and safety stock levels
  • Item photos you capture or choose, plus sticker images generated from them
  • Replacement, restock, snooze, and usage records
  • Shopping list content and app settings

3. Firebase Analytics（匿名）3. Firebase Analytics (Anonymous)

應用程式使用 Firebase Analytics 收集匿名功能使用事件，協助我們了解功能是否正常、改善產品體驗。事件可能包含 onboarding、建立或編輯物品、標記更換、補貨、延後、使用模板、建立貼紙、購物清單操作、雲端備份觸發、通知開關、paywall 顯示、訂閱購買等。

The app uses Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous feature usage events so we can understand whether features work as expected and improve the product. Events may include onboarding, creating or editing items, marking replacements, restocking, snoozing, using templates, creating stickers, shopping list actions, cloud backup triggers, notification settings, paywall views, and subscription purchases.

這些事件不包含物品名稱、照片內容、個人識別資訊或付款資訊。Firebase 的資料處理受 Google Firebase 隱私權與安全性說明管轄。

These events do not include item names, photo contents, personally identifying information, or payment information. Firebase data processing is governed by Google Firebase privacy and security information.

4. 雲端備份（選用）4. Cloud Backup (Optional)

雲端備份為選用功能，需要登入帳號後由你主動觸發。登入方式可能支援 Apple、Google 或平台提供的登入方式。

Cloud backup is optional and must be triggered by you after signing in. Sign-in may use Apple, Google, or platform-provided sign-in methods.

使用雲端備份時，物品資料與貼紙圖片可能會上傳至 Firebase Storage，並與你的帳號關聯。雲端最多保留最近 3 份備份；你可以登出並聯絡我們要求刪除雲端備份資料。

When you use cloud backup, item data and sticker images may be uploaded to Firebase Storage and associated with your account. Up to the latest three backups are retained. You may sign out and contact us to request deletion of cloud backup data.

5. 意見回饋5. Feedback

當你透過 App 內的意見回饋功能提交問題或建議時，以下資料可能會上傳至 Firebase Firestore：

When you submit feedback in the app, the following information may be uploaded to Firebase Firestore:

  • 你填寫的回饋內容與選填 Email
  • App 版本、訂閱方案、裝置型號、系統版本與裝置名稱
  • 若已登入：Firebase 帳號 UID 與信箱
  • Your feedback message and optional email address
  • App version, subscription plan, device model, system version, and device name
  • If signed in: Firebase account UID and email address

這些資料僅用於處理你的回饋，不會用於廣告或出售。

This information is used only to process your feedback. It is not used for advertising or sold.

6. 權限與裝置功能6. Permissions and Device Features

通知

Notifications

應用程式會請求通知權限，用於在物品到期或備品低於安全庫存時提醒你。通知內容由裝置本機產生，不會傳送至任何伺服器。

The app may request notification permission to remind you when items are due or inventory is below your safety stock level. Notification content is generated locally on your device and is not sent to a server.

相機與照片

Camera and Photos

當你為物品拍照或從相片庫選取圖片時，應用程式會請求相機或相片庫存取權限。照片在本機處理；僅在你主動觸發雲端備份時，貼紙圖片才可能同步至 Firebase Storage。

When you take a photo or choose an image for an item, the app may request camera or photo library access. Photos are processed locally. Sticker images may sync to Firebase Storage only when you trigger cloud backup.

生物辨識 / 系統驗證

Biometric / System Authentication

App Lock 可使用裝置支援的生物辨識或系統驗證方式。生物辨識資料由作業系統管理，應用程式不會存取、儲存或傳輸你的生物特徵資料。

App Lock can use biometric or system authentication supported by your device. Biometric data is managed by the operating system; the app does not access, store, or transmit your biometric data.

7. 使用的第三方服務7. Third-Party Services

應用程式可能整合以下服務：

The app may integrate the following services:

  • Firebase Analytics — 匿名功能使用事件
  • Firebase Authentication — Apple / Google 或平台登入
  • Firebase Storage — 雲端備份儲存
  • Cloud Firestore — 意見回饋儲存
  • Firebase Remote Config — 應用程式設定與版本控制
  • Apple App Store / StoreKit 與 Google Play Billing — 訂閱、內購、復原購買與訂閱狀態
  • Firebase Analytics — anonymous feature usage events
  • Firebase Authentication — Apple / Google or platform sign-in
  • Firebase Storage — cloud backup storage
  • Cloud Firestore — feedback storage
  • Firebase Remote Config — app settings and version control
  • Apple App Store / StoreKit and Google Play Billing — subscriptions, in-app purchases, purchase restoration, and subscription status

8. 兒童隱私與政策變更8. Children's Privacy and Changes

本應用程式不針對 13 歲以下兒童設計。若你發現兒童在未經允許下提交了個人資料，請聯絡我們，我們將協助處理。

The app is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child submitted personal information without permission, contact us and we will help address it.

若本政策有所變更，我們會在此頁更新內容並修改最後更新日期。重大變更可能透過 App 內訊息、商店資訊或版本更新說明告知。

If this policy changes, we will update this page and the last updated date. Material changes may be announced in the app, store listing, or release notes.
