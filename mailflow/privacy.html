Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

MailFlow ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles your information.

Key Point: MailFlow is a 100% local application. Your emails and personal data are stored only on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.

1. Information We Access

When you sign in with your Google account, MailFlow accesses:

  • Email messages: Subject, sender, recipients, body content, and attachments
  • Email metadata: Labels, read/unread status, timestamps
  • Basic profile: Your email address and name (for display purposes only)

2. How We Use Your Data

Your Gmail data is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Displaying your emails in a conversation-style interface
  • Organizing emails by sender for easier management
  • Syncing read/unread status, labels, and archive actions with Gmail
  • Enabling search functionality within your emails

3. Data Storage and Security

Local-Only Architecture

MailFlow operates with a local-only architecture:

  • All email data is stored in a secure database on your device
  • Your OAuth tokens are stored in the iOS Keychain (encrypted by the operating system)
  • We do not operate any backend servers that store or process your data
  • Your emails are never transmitted to us or any third party

Security Measures

  • OAuth 2.0 authentication (we never see or store your Google password)
  • iOS Keychain for secure credential storage
  • App sandboxing provided by iOS
  • All communications with Gmail use HTTPS/TLS encryption

4. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect or store your emails on our servers
  • We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties
  • We do not use your data for advertising or profiling
  • We do not use your data for AI/ML training
  • We do not track your usage behavior

5. Google API Services - Limited Use Disclosure

MailFlow's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Specifically, MailFlow:

  • Only uses Gmail data to provide the email client functionality described in this policy
  • Does not transfer Gmail data to third parties
  • Does not use Gmail data for advertising purposes
  • Does not allow humans to read your Gmail data (unless with your explicit consent for support purposes, or as required by law)

Requested Permission Scope

MailFlow requests the following Google OAuth scope:

  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/gmail.modify

This permission allows the App to:

  • Read your email messages and metadata
  • Mark emails as read/unread
  • Archive, trash, and restore emails
  • Add or remove stars from emails

6. Data Retention

  • On your device: Email data is retained until you delete it or uninstall the App
  • On our servers: Not applicable - we do not store any of your data

7. Your Rights and Choices

Revoking Access

You can revoke MailFlow's access to your Gmail at any time:

  1. Visit Google Account Permissions
  2. Find "MailFlow" in the list of apps
  3. Click "Remove Access"

Deleting Your Data

To delete all locally stored data:

  • Uninstall the MailFlow app from your device, or
  • Use the "Clear Data" option in the App settings (if available)

Since we do not store any data on servers, uninstalling the app removes all data associated with MailFlow.

8. Children's Privacy

MailFlow is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
